# Contrast review checklist

Review each report page against these items and record the result in the
design-review evidence. Guidance only -- it never grants a readiness pass.

- Body text vs its background reaches at least WCAG AA (4.5:1).
- Large titles vs background reach at least 3:1.
- Meaning is never carried by color alone (pair color with a label or icon).
- Sequential ramps stay distinguishable in grayscale.
- Status colors (good/neutral/bad) remain distinct for common color-vision
  deficiencies.
