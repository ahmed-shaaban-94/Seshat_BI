# Sales and Revenue Domain

Top-line value generated by the business. This domain anchors the gross-vs-net and VAT
conventions every other domain inherits.

## KPIs in this domain

| KPI | Contract | Status |
|-----|----------|--------|
| Gross Sales | `contracts/gross-sales.md` | Seeded |
| Net Sales | `contracts/net-sales.md` | Seeded |
| Quantity Sold | `contracts/quantity-sold.md` | Seeded |
| Average Transaction Value | `contracts/average-transaction-value.md` | Seeded |
| Net Sales Growth % | — | Planned |
| Sales per Square Meter | — | Planned (needs store floor-area field) |

## Key ambiguities (see knowledge/kpi-ambiguities.md)

- A1 VAT included vs excluded — assume pre-tax unless owner states otherwise.
- A4 Gross vs net — never substitute one for the other.
- A3 Sale date vs posting date — name the primary date in each contract.
- A2 Returns handling — decide netted vs separate before reporting revenue.

## Owner

Finance (primary), with Sales / Commercial as stakeholders.

## Notes

Net Sales is the base for most derived retail KPIs (margin, ATV, sales per sqm, growth,
vs target). Define it cleanly first; reuse it everywhere rather than re-deriving.
